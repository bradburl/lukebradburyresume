I am a 2x All-State, 2x State Champion, 3x Conference Champion, and National/High School Team Captain.

Why Volleyball?

Volleyball has given me more than just medals and trophies. The connections, trust, and friendships I made with my teammates are what I value most. We had a great group of boys, and the memories we created together are more important than any success I had in the sport.
